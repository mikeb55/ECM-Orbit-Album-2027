## Core Concept — Mirror Inversion

**Reflective refraction** (not axial destabilization).

- Motivic inversion drives structure.
- No tritone rupture.
- No delayed axis shock.

---

## CELL

**Base CELL (concert):** C – Db – G – B

**Profile:** m2 + P4 + M7

**Flugelhorn (written):** D – Eb – A – C#

| Section | Transformation |
|---------|----------------|
| A | Stated normally |
| B | Intervallic inversion (B–G–Db–C) |
| A' | Registral mirror |
| Return | Compressed inversion |
| Coda | Retrograde fragment only |

No scalar embellishment.

---

## Harmonic Strategy

- Parallel harmonic planes
- Major triad over unrelated bass
- Minor 2 cluster colors
- Static harmony with registral displacement
- Bass movement is registral, not harmonic
- Avoid strong pedal identity
- Avoid tonal gravity

---

## Texture

- **Guitar:** Refractive surface — harmonics, open intervals, slow inner voice shifts
- **Bass:** Registral shifts; avoid obvious root reinforcement
- **Flugel:** Mid-register dominant; one high-register moment only (M51)

No comping.

---

## Coda

- Non-root color tone over ambiguous bass
- Prefer 9 or suspended 4
- No tonal closure
