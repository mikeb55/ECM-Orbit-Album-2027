## HABs (Harmonic Atmosphere Blocks)

### HAB1 (Bars 1–16)
- **Bass axis:** Single pedal G2
- **Guitar structures:** Quartal (G–C–D), maj7#11 (F#–A), min(add9) (G–Bb), quartal (D–G)
- **Rules:** No II–V–I; voice-leading economical; space for flugel

### HAB2 (Bars 17–34)
- **Bass axis:** Tritone shift → C#2 (from G)
- **Guitar structures:** Maj7#11 (G–C#), quartal (C#–F#), cluster (G–B)
- **Common tone:** G (present in both HAB1 and HAB2 guitar voicings)

---

## CELL

**Intervallic cell (concert pitch):** D4 – G4 – Ab4 – C#5

| Interval | Type |
|----------|------|
| D4 → G4 | P4 |
| G4 → Ab4 | m2 |
| Ab4 → C#5 | M7 (from D4) |

**Flugelhorn (written):** E4 – A4 – Bb4 – D#5

**Transformations used:**
- **Head (A):** Full cell, bars 1–2
- **B:** Sustain + truncation
- **C:** Transposed to C# field (F#4 – B4 – C5 – F5)
- **Open:** Fragments (max 3 notes/bar)
- **Return:** Inversion (D#5 – Bb4 – A4 – E4)
- **Coda:** Fragment (E4 sustained)

## Plane Shift

- **From:** G pedal (HAB1)  
- **To:** C# pedal (HAB2)  
- **Common tone:** G (in guitar voicings across both fields)  
- **CELL:** Persists transposed; intervals P4, m2, M7 maintained
